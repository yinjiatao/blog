## Why Email Lists Still Matter

Social media algorithms are constantly changing:

- Organic reach on platforms keeps declining
- Recommendation mechanisms are opaque
- Open rates for newsletter platforms keep dropping

But email:

- Reaches users' inboxes directly
- Has relatively stable open rates
- Is entirely under your control
- Has high conversion rates

## The Value of an Email List

### 1. You Own Your Audience

Social media accounts can be banned, but your email list is your asset.

### 2. Deep Connection

Email is a private, one-to-one communication method that makes it easier to build trust.

### 3. High Conversion

Email marketing conversion rates are typically 10x higher than social media.

## How to Build an Email List

### 1. Choose Your Tool

- **ConvertKit**: creator-friendly, powerful automation
- **Mailchimp**: full-featured, generous free tier
- **Substack**: content + email in one

### 2. Create a Lead Magnet

Offer value in exchange for an email address:

- Free ebook
- Useful templates
- Exclusive content

### 3. Set Up Subscription Forms

Placement options:

- End of articles
- Website sidebar
- Dedicated landing page

## How to Operate Your Email List

### Sending Frequency

Find the balance:

- Too frequent → unsubscribes
- Too sparse → forgotten

Recommended: once per week, at a fixed time.

### Content Strategy

80% value, 20% promotion:

- Exclusive content
- Deep thinking
- Practical tips
- Personal stories

### Improving Open Rates

1. **Optimize subject lines**: curious, specific, urgent
2. **Send time**: test what works best for your audience
3. **Clean your list**: regularly remove inactive subscribers
4. **Personalization**: use subscribers' names

## Getting Started

1. Choose an email tool
2. Create a lead magnet
3. Set up a subscription form
4. Write your first welcome email
5. Develop a content plan

Don't wait until you have 1,000 subscribers to start. Begin with 10 and grow gradually.
